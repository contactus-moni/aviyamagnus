About this Course

The Finance & Accounting course helps you master the principles of financial management, budgeting, investment analysis, and accounting fundamentals. You’ll gain the skills to interpret financial data, make informed business decisions, and manage organizational resources efficiently.

Key Modules

  • ✅ Financial Reporting and Analysis
  • ✅ Budgeting, Forecasting & Cost Control
  • ✅ Corporate Finance and Investment Decisions
  • ✅ Accounting Principles & Standards (GAAP / IFRS)
  • ✅ Taxation, Auditing & Risk Management
  • ✅ Financial Planning and Decision Making

Course Duration & Certification

Duration: 6 to 9 Months | Mode: Online / Hybrid

Upon completion, participants will receive a recognized certification from Aviya Magnus, validating their expertise in Finance & Acounting.
