Trade & Commerce

Empower your career by mastering business principles, trade operations, and global market strategies.

About this Course

The Trade & Commerce course is designed to build a solid understanding of domestic and international trade systems, market structures, business transactions, and economic principles. You’ll learn how goods and services move through the marketplace and how trade policies and commercial strategies shape business success globally.

Key Modules

  • ✅ Principles of Trade and Commerce
  • ✅ International Business and Global Markets
  • ✅ Import–Export Management
  • ✅ Business Law and Trade Regulations
  • ✅ E-Commerce and Digital Trade
  • ✅ Supply Chain and Logistics Fundamentals

Course Duration & Certification

Duration: 6 to 9 Months | Mode: Online / Hybrid

Upon completion, participants will receive a recognized certification from Aviya Magnus, validating their expertise in Trade & Commerce.
