Photography & Videography

Empower your career by mastering photography techniques, video production, and creative storytelling.

About this Course

The Photography & Videography course equips you with the skills to capture stunning images, create compelling videos, and tell stories visually. Learn technical techniques, editing, lighting, and composition to bring your creative vision to life.

Key Modules

  • ✅ Fundamentals of Photography: Composition, Lighting & Camera Settings
  • ✅ Portrait, Landscape & Product Photography
  • ✅ Videography Techniques: Shooting, Angles & Motion
  • ✅ Video Editing & Post-Production (Software: Premiere, Final Cut, etc.)
  • ✅ Storyboarding & Creative Storytelling
  • ✅ Portfolio Development & Professional Presentation

Course Duration & Certification

Duration: 6 to 9 Months | Mode: Online / Hybrid

Upon completion, participants will receive a recognized certification from Aviya Magnus, validating their expertise in Photography & Videography.
