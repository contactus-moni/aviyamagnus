About Aviya Magnus

About Us

Aviya Magnus is a forward-thinking educational platform dedicated to empowering learners across all age groups with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed — both in academics and in life.

We offer personalized tutoring services for students at the primary, secondary, and higher secondary levels, supporting all major education boards including CBSE, ICSE, State Boards, and international curricula like IB and IGCSE. Our experienced educators focus on building strong conceptual understanding, exam readiness, and confidence through a blend of offline and online classes tailored to each student’s pace and goals.

In addition to academic tutoring, we provide foundation skill courses such as:

  • 💻 Basics of Computer
  • 🗣️ Spoken English & Communication Skills
  • ✍️ Study Skills & Soft Skills for Students

To support lifelong learning and career advancement, Aviya Magnus also offers professional certification courses in key non-technical fields, including:

  • Human Resources (HR)
  • Finance & Accounting
  • Banking & Financial Service
  • Marketing & Business Strategy
  • Office Administration & Business Communication

These certification programs are ideal for college students, job seekers, and working professionals looking to upskill, reskill, or pursue new career paths in today’s competitive job market.

At Aviya Magnus, we believe education is more than just grades — it’s about empowering learners to grow academically, personally, and professionally. Whether you're a school student, a college graduate, or a young professional, we’re here to support your journey every step of the way.

Vision

"To be a trusted leader in modern education by empowering every learner with personalized, engaging, and accessible tutoring that shapes confident, lifelong achievers."

Mission

"At Aviya Magnus, our mission is to unlock each student’s full potential by delivering high-quality, individualized tutoring—both online and offline—guided by expert educators, innovative tools, and a passion for making learning inspiring and results-driven."
