Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our programs, partnerships, and services.

1. What is Aviya Magnus?

Aviya Magnus is a professional tutoring and certification platform that offers practical, career-focused courses in computer basics, spoken English, finance, HR, business management, banking, commerce, trading, and skill development.

2. Who can join your courses?

Our courses are designed for students, fresh graduates, job seekers, working professionals, and anyone looking to improve their skills or career opportunities.

3. Do you provide online or offline classes?

We provide both online and offline classes to suit your convenience. You can choose the mode that works best for you

4. What is the duration of the courses?

Most basic certification courses range from 1 to 3 months, depending on the subject and learning pace.

5. Will I receive a certificate after completing the course?

Yes, all students receive a recognized certification from Aviya Magnus after successful completion of their course.

6. Are your courses practical or only theory-based?

Our training is hands-on and practical. We focus on real-world applications, case studies, and live examples to ensure you can use your skills immediately..

7. How are the classes conducted online?

Online classes are conducted through Zoom/Google Meet with live interaction, recorded sessions (where available), and digital study materials.

8. Do you offer demo classes?

Yes! We offer free demo classes so you can experience our teaching style before enrolling.

9. What are the fees for your courses?

WCourse fees vary based on the program. Please contact us directly for detailed fee structures and any ongoing offers..

10. Can working professionals join your courses?

Absolutely! Our flexible schedules and online options make it easy for working professionals to upgrade their skills.

11. Do you provide placement support?

Yes, we provide career guidance, resume building, and interview preparation support. While we don’t guarantee jobs, many of our students have successfully secured opportunities through our network.

12. What makes Aviya Magnus different from others?

Our focus is on practical training, personalized attention, and career readiness. We don’t just teach subjects — we prepare you for real-world challenges.

13. How can I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by filling out the enrollment form on our website, contacting us via phone/WhatsApp, or visiting our office for offline registration.

14. Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide digital notes, assignments, and practice exercises for every course. Some programs also include downloadable templates and case studies.
